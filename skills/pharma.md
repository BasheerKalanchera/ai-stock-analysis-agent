# Pharmaceuticals & Biotech Valuation Skill

## Core Financials (From Excel)
- **P/E vs Historical Average**: 20-35x depending on formulation vs API mix.
- **EBITDA / OPM (%)**: > 18% is strong; > 25% indicates proprietary/complex portfolio.
- **ROCE (%)**: > 15% demonstrates efficient R&D and manufacturing capital allocation.
- **Debt-to-Equity**: < 0.5 is ideal.
- **Sales CAGR vs OPM Spread**: Strong top-line growth mixed with stable margins proves pricing power against generic competition.

## Strategy & Narrative KPIs (From PPT)
- **Geography Mix / Export Markets**: US (high margin/high regulatory risk) vs Domestic India vs Rest of World.
- **R&D Pipeline / ANDA Filings**: Visibility on new complex generic launches.
- **USFDA Regulatory Updates**: Clean facility inspections (EIR/NAI) vs Warning Letters. 

## Valuation Rules
- Pure Domestic formulations command a steady, FMCG-like defensive P/E premium.
- US-heavy export portfolios are volatile — discount P/E for FDA compliance risks, but upgrade if complex generic pipeline is robust.
- Watch R&D spend (percentage of sales) – scaling back R&D artificially boosts current margins but kills future growth.

## Governance Check
- FDA OAI (Official Action Indicated) or Import Alerts are extreme operational red flags.
- Promoter pledging < 5% is acceptable.

## Secondary / Deep Research Checks (Check filings if needed)
- Exact R&D spend as % of revenue, precise breakdown of complex vs simple generics, and litigation/patent settlements.
