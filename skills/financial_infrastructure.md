# Financial Infrastructure & AMCs Valuation Skill

## Core Financials (From Excel)
- **P/E vs Historical Average**: Usually commands a steep premium (30-50x) due to monopolies/duopolies.
- **EBITDA / OPM (%)**: Expected to be extremely high (> 40%), showcasing operating leverage.
- **ROCE & ROE (%)**: > 30% is expected for asset-light cash machines.
- **CFO/PAT Conversion**: > 90%; these businesses rarely have severe working capital needs.
- **Dividend Payout Ratio**: High dividend payouts expected; retained cash has few reinvestment avenues.

## Strategy & Narrative KPIs (From PPT)
- **AUM Growth (AMCs/Wealth)**: Total Assets Under Management is the core revenue driver.
- **Market Share / Moat (Exchanges/Depositories)**: Narrative around transaction volumes, active client additions, or duopoly dominance.

## Valuation Rules
- **P/E is paramount.** These are pure-play compounding platforms.
- Revenue growth flows almost entirely to the bottom line (high operating leverage).
- Check for regulatory risks: AMCs face SEBI TER (Total Expense Ratio) cuts; Exchanges face transaction fee structure changes.

## Governance Check
- Any regulatory actions or fines mentioned in recent quarters.
- Promoter pledging > 0% is a RED FLAG.

## Secondary / Deep Research Checks (Check filings if needed)
- Exact Yields/TER realizations (AMCs) or precise transaction volume splits (Exchanges).
