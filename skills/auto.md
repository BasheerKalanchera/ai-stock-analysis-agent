# Auto OEMs Valuation Skill

## Core Financials (From Excel)
- **P/E vs Historical Average**: 15-25x is fair for established ICE OEMs; higher allowed if transitioning structure to EV.
- **Sales CAGR vs OPM Spread**: Shows operating leverage. Rapid Sales growth + improving Margins = Premium.
- **EBITDA / OPM (%)**: > 12% is strong for OEMs; > 15% is best-in-class pricing power.
- **ROCE (%)**: > 15% is strong given heavy manufacturing capital intensity.
- **Debt-to-Equity**: Must be < 1.0 (excluding captive finance arms).
- **CFO/PAT Conversion**: > 80% is expected. Poor conversion signals inventory pile-ups at dealers.

## Strategy & Narrative KPIs (From PPT)
- **Market Share Trend**: Gaining vs losing market share in key segments shapes the narrative.
- **EV Transition / Pipeline**: Commentary on upcoming launches and EV optionality.

## Valuation Rules
- **P/E for steady-state OEMs.**
- Volume growth + market share gains = deserves PE premium over peers.
- EV transition capex depresses near-term margins — discount for transition cost but add option value for EV pipeline.

## Governance Check
- MNC subsidiaries: track royalty payments to parent — rising royalties dilute value.
- Promoter pledging < 3% acceptable.

## Secondary / Deep Research Checks (Check filings if needed)
- Absolute unit volume growth % vs industry, and exact Export Revenue % split.
