# IT Services & Software Valuation Skill

## Core Financials (From Excel)
- **P/E vs Historical Average**: 20-30x is standard for Large Caps; Mid-Caps can command 30-50x if growth > 20%.
- **Revenue CAGR**: > 10% for large caps, > 15% for mid-caps indicates market share gains.
- **EBITDA / OPM (%)**: > 20% indicates strong pricing power; > 25% is an elite tier (TCS, etc.).
- **ROCE (%)**: > 25% is expected; this is an asset-lite, people-heavy business.
- **CFO/PAT Conversion**: > 80% is critical. IT companies should be cash machines.

## Strategy & Narrative KPIs (From PPT)
- **Total Contract Value (TCV) / Deal Wins**: Large deal announcements show revenue visibility.
- **Attrition Rate Narrative**: Declining or stable attrition protects margins.
- **Client Additions / Revenue Brackets**: Growth in $50M+ or $100M+ client buckets.

## Valuation Rules
- P/E expansion is driven by TCV pipeline and margin resilience.
- Sub-contracting costs (if visible in margins) indicate talent supply issues.
- Cash on books: IT firms accumulate massive cash piles. Look for buybacks or high dividend payouts.

## Governance Check
- Promoter pledging > 0% is extraordinarily rare in IT Services and an immediate RED FLAG.

## Secondary / Deep Research Checks (Check filings if needed)
- Exact Attrition % numbers, Utilization rates, and Revenue by Vertical (BFSI, Retail, etc.).
