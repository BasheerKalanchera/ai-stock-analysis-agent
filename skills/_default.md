# General Valuation Skill

## Core Financials (From Excel)
- **P/E vs Historical Average**: Assess if current P/E is trading at a premium/discount to its 3-year or 5-year median.
- **PEG Ratio**: < 1.0 is deeply undervalued; 1.0 - 2.0 is fair for compounders; > 2.0 requires high ROCE to justify.
- **ROCE (%)**: > 15% demonstrates strong capital allocation; > 25% is an elite compounder.
- **Operating Margin Trend (OPM)**: Improving YoY/QoQ signals pricing power; declining margins signal cost pressure.
- **Debt-to-Equity**: < 0.5 preferred; > 1.0 requires stable cash flows.
- **CFO/PAT Conversion**: > 80% confirms high-quality earnings and efficient working capital; < 50% is a major red flag for aggressive accounting.

## Strategy & Narrative KPIs (From PPT)
- **Revenue Growth / Market Share**: Focus on directional narrative (gaining or losing share?).
- **Management Guidance/Outlook**: Key forward-looking catalysts driving the premium/discount.

## Valuation Rules
- P/E expansion must be backed by ROCE expansion or significant Debt reduction.
- Premium valuations (>40x P/E) demand CFO/PAT > 80% and ROCE > 20%.

## Governance Check
- Promoter pledging > 0% is a RED FLAG.

## Secondary / Deep Research Checks (Check filings if needed)
- Exact geographical revenue splits or client concentration risks.
